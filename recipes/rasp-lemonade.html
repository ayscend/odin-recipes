About Raspberry Lemonade

Raspberry Lemonade is a simple twist on regular lemonade that just makes it a much more interesting drink. It's the perfect summer drink you can serve to anybody and keep you hydrated through hot days.

Ingredients for Raspberry Lemonade(1 gallon)

  • 12 oz fresh raspberries
  • 10 fresh lemons
  • 1 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp salt

    • Tools

  • Food Processor
  • Mesh strainer/sift
  • Citrus Juicer
  • A large 1 gallon pitcher

Steps

  1. Chop 8 lemons in halves, then juice them with the citrus juicer. This should yield 1 1/4 cups of lemon juice.
  2. Rinse your raspberries in the sift.
  3. Put all raspberries in food processor, and blend until smooth.
  4. Get a large pot on medium heat, and add 3 quarts of water.
  5. Add in the lemon juice(including seeds and pulp) along with the blended raspberries.
  6. Add 1 1/4 cup of sugar and 1 tsp of salt in the pot too.
  7. Stir ingredients in pot for 8-10 minutes on medium heat.
  8. After 8-10 minutes, turn off the heat and cover with a lid. Leave it for 30 minutes.
  9. Get a large bowl, and use a strainer while pouring it into the bowl.
  10. Throw out the rest of the pulp in the strainer.
  11. Pour the raspberry lemonade into a pitcher.
  12. You can put it in your fridge to cool it off for the next day.
  13. Serve in a glass with some ice and fresh lemon slices and raspberries!
